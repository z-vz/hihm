About Me

Hi, my name is Zac and I’ve been working in the building industry for over 15 years. I have extensive knowledge in all aspects of maintenance and construction and I’d love to help you out on your next project or repair.

I’ve completed all manner of projects before. From renovating my own home to working on large scale commercial projects. I understand time constraints, I’m always on time and I’m happy to fit my work around your schedules.

What I can offer

  • General Maintenance

    From leaky roofs to cracked tiles. I’ve seen it all and can fix almost anything.

  • Painting and Decorating

    Whether you’ve got holes in the walls or just want a fresh lick of paint. I can make it look as good as new.

  • Construction

    New decks, Handrails, Fences, Gates etc. If you’ve got a renovation project you want sorted I can hep you out

  • Flat Pack Installation

    New Ikea table or a set of drawers. I’ll put together flat pack furniture in no time at all.

  • Pickup or Delivery

    Need something picked up or delivered? Let me know and I’ll be happy to help out.

  • Tech Help

    I’m very much a modern handyman and keep up to date with modern technology and can help install that new TV or printer.

This is only a snippet of my skills as a handyman.

If you can imagine it I can probably do it.
